## 重点辨析

**impact vs influence（两个都是"影响"，有区别）**

| 词 | 感觉 | 例子 |
|----|------|------|
| impact | 强烈冲击，通常是短期的、可感知的 | The earthquake had a devastating impact. |
| influence | 潜移默化，通常是长期的、渐进的 | Her teacher had a great influence on her. |

**suggest 有两种用法（考试常考）**

1. 建议：suggest + doing sth（注意：不是 suggest to do）
   - Experts suggest **reducing** screen time. ✅
   - Experts suggest **to reduce** screen time. ❌

2. 表明（research/data 做主语）：suggest + that 从句
   - Research suggests **that** exercise improves mood. ✅
