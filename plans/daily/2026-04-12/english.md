## 今日场景：面试高管岗位

想象你在面试一家公司的管理岗位，面试官在描述职责：

> "这个职位需要你 **analyze**（分析）市场数据，**identify**（识别）潜在风险，**implement**（执行）新方案，**coordinate**（协调）跨部门团队，并持续 **evaluate**（评估）结果。你还需要 **demonstrate**（展示）你的 **relevant**（相关的）经验，**emphasize**（强调）你的 **specific**（具体的）成就，证明你能为公司 **contribute**（贡献）价值，并 **maintain**（保持）高标准。"

## 今日词表

| 单词 | 词性 | 中文 | 例句（考研级别）|
|------|------|------|----------------|
| analyze | v | 分析 | We need to **analyze** the data carefully before drawing conclusions. |
| identify | v | 识别、确定 | Scientists have **identified** a new risk factor for the disease. |
| implement | v | 实施、执行 | The company plans to **implement** the new policy next month. |
| coordinate | v | 协调 | She was asked to **coordinate** the efforts of different departments. |
| evaluate | v | 评估 | It is important to **evaluate** the results of any experiment. |
| demonstrate | v | 展示、证明 | The study **demonstrates** that exercise improves mental health. |
| relevant | adj | 相关的 | Please provide only **relevant** information in your application. |
| emphasize | v | 强调 | The report **emphasizes** the need for immediate action. |
| specific | adj | 具体的、特定的 | Can you give a more **specific** example? |
| contribute | v | 贡献、促成 | Many factors **contribute** to the success of a business. |

## 记忆技巧

**词根联想：**
- **analyze** → analysis（名词），analytical（形容词）→ 一个词学一族
- **identify** → identity（身份）→ 识别身份
- **implement** → implementation（名词）→ 执行/落实
- **coordinate** → coordination → co（共同）+ ordinate（有序）
- **evaluate** → evaluation → eval + value → 给出价值判断
- **demonstrate** → demonstration → demo（演示的正式说法）
- **relevant** → irrelevant（不相关的）→ 考试常用反义词
- **emphasize** → emphasis（名词）→ place emphasis on = emphasize
- **specific** → specifically（副词）→ in specific terms（具体来说）
- **contribute** → contribution → contributor（贡献者）
